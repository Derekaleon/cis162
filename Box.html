About UWllness

My companys goal

In recent years, a heavy emphasis has been placed on our physical and mental well-being; however, there is limited availability to clean, sustainable products that lead to long, healthy lives. With this shift in trends towards holistic solutions, my brand aims to increase accessibility of preventative health measures to enhance livelihoods.

The objective of my e-commerce site is to deliver health and wellness products to customers' doorsteps when they subscribe to my service. When my customers subscribe to my website, they will be able to customize their own health and wellness box with any healthy product they want delivered. The contents inside these boxes can range from soaps, candles, healthy snacks, to skincare products, jewelry, books, mindfulness products, vitamins and more.

FAQ

Where are your products from?
We obtain our products from high end companies, such as bath and body works, NIVEA, Aveeno, Dove, Barnes and Noble, and many others. You will only be putting high quality products into your personal wellness box.

At the moment we are just a online e-commerce business whos staff work from home. We do not have a headquarters nor warehouse.
